How to Help

These programs are driven by community members willing to step up and keep our mission to reduce the dependency of pay-to-play programs in our areas. There are many ways that you can be a part of this change.

Donations:
We believe in a 100% transparency policy. This means that instead of taking cash donations, we promote the donation of essential items to keep these programs alive.

Volunteer:
Our programs are run by volunteers! Not only are you helping promote healthy lifestyles and keeping our programs free, but for high school volunteers, all hours can be used towards graduation cords and NHS points. Please note that all volunteers are encouraged to attend the volunteer orientation at the beginning of summer!

Spread the Word:
We are trying to promote our programs to increase our prevalence in our community. Don't only tell your friends about PGM, bring them to our events!

What is PGM?


Project Get Moving is a community-based campaign to encourage everyone to... get moving! Our goal is to create valuable opportunities that anyone and everyone can take part in, reducing the dependability of pay-to-play programs. All opportunities are completely free of cost and run through a team of dedicated volunteers who all understand the importance of the positive effects the outdoors has on individuals.

The following opportunities are what is currently offered by PGM:

Free Summer Camp

Free Movie Nights in the Park

Summer Chalk Challenge
